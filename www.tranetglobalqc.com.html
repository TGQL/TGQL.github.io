Who we are...

TRANET GLOBAL QC LIMITED (TGQL) is a Nigerian company registered as a private limited liability company under the Company and Allied Matters Act 1990.

We are a one-stop firm for all your quality management system needs. Our Quality Management System Consultancy Services including but not limited to Training and Certification of Personnel, Quality Management System Auditing, Documentation, Accreditation and Certification Guidance.

We also carry out Quality Control and Inspection services to the Oil and Gas Industry in Nigeria and Africa.
Our personnel are highly experienced and certified to various International and Industry certifications covering all aspect of our services.

TRANET GLOBAL QC LIMITED (TGQL) is in corporation with various Nigerian and International Technical Bodies, Management System Certification and Accreditation Bodies, in order to aid our clients actualize their quality goals.

Our main target is to deliver the highest quality services to our clients within and beyond Africa; in pursuant of the above we remain strategically aligned to various Technical Partners, Certification/ Accreditation Bodies locally and globally to ensure up-to date information of all applicable requirements.  

Our Vision

To be one of the leading Quality Management System Consultancy Firm Globally 

Mission

To assure superior quality to our Clients via Quality Control & Inspection Services throughout the Oil and Gas sector within Africa and beyond. Consequently we are committed to conscious Implementation of our Management System in line with ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 17020:2012; adherence with best Industry Practices including applicable Industry Health and Safety Standards in our operations.
